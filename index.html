What is MedicTELL?

MedicTELL is a platform that allows nurses to interact with people. It is also designed for nurses, people with disabilities and the elderly to promote interaction remotely, allowing them to book appointments online and get a job online.

What are the benefits?

  • Remote interaction
  • Book appointments online
  • Receive recommendations from your favorite doctors
  • Have an extension of nurses
  • Be connected 24 hours and more

What functions can you have once registered?

For nurses:

  • Diary
  • configurable schedule
  • Forums
  • and much mores

For the general public:

  • online appointment booking
  • Diary
  • queries
  • and much more
