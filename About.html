What is NewTEC?

We are NewTec, a group of students from the Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas that seeks to guide relatives or those involved who are under their care of people with disabilities and/or elderly people through the use of web and mobile applications.

Mission

“Improving the quality of life of people with disabilities and dependent elderly people”

Vision

“To be one of the best companies that help people who suffer from health problems”

Values

  • Empathy
  • Commitment
  • Innovation
